At CeyQuest, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to protecting the data and personal information of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with CeyQuest.

1. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide and improve our services:

Personal Information:
When you sign up or interact with CeyQuest, we may collect personal details like your name, email address, and other contact information.

Usage Data:
We gather data on how you use CeyQuest, including search history, question logs, preferences, and interactions with learning tools.

Device Information:
We collect data about the device you use to access CeyQuest, such as your IP address, browser type, device model, and operating system.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to enhance your learning experience and improve our services:

  • Service Delivery: Your data helps us provide personalized content, relevant suggestions, and features tailored to your educational needs.

  • Improvement and Development: We analyze usage data to improve CeyQuest’s features, content accuracy, and functionality.

  • Customer Support: If you contact us for support, we may use your information to help resolve your questions or concerns.

  • Security: We use information to maintain the safety and security of CeyQuest, including detecting suspicious activities or abuse.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share data under the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your data with trusted third-party vendors who help us operate CeyQuest (e.g., hosting, analytics, or customer support).

  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to lawful requests.

  • Business Transfers: In case of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred as part of the business assets.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. However, no online service can guarantee complete security.

5. Your Data Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Access: Request access to the personal data we hold about you.

  • Correction: Ask for corrections if your information is inaccurate or incomplete.

  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to any legal requirements.

  • Opt-Out: You can choose to limit certain types of data usage, such as personalized recommendations.

6. Third-Party Links

CeyQuest may contain links to external websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

7. Children’s Privacy

CeyQuest is primarily designed for students with parental or school guidance. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without appropriate consent. If we discover such information has been collected, we will take steps to delete it.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a new effective date. We encourage you to review it periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
