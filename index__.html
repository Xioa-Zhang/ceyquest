About CeyQuest

Why You Absolutely Should Join the CeyQuest Learning Revolution

CeyQuest is Sri Lanka’s first AI-powered education platform designed to empower students through smart, syllabus-aligned learning. Whether you're a Grade 6 student or preparing for the O/L exam, CeyQuest brings you a personalized and tech-driven way to study. Built with modern learners in mind, it combines advanced AI, subject-specific resources, and gamified progress tracking to make education more effective and engaging.


From real-time Q&A with CeynovX AI to interactive quizzes, video lessons, and a personalized dashboard — CeyQuest gives students everything they need to stay motivated, focused, and ahead. Whether you're aiming for top ranks, supporting your class as a teacher, or helping your child as a parent, CeyQuest is more than a platform — it's the future of learning in your hands.
